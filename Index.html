Polymer and Composites

Everyday we use different plastics in various forms and size, within our household and work environment. I have been working in plastics (polymer) industry for a good 10 years, helping to design, develop and improve products that are used in packaging, telecommunications and electrical applications.

Cable Insulation

Electrical wires in household and office are something we dont see everyday but we are surrounded by them. Copper is used as conductor in these wires which are surrounded by insulating polymers as a jacket. These jackets vary in their characteristics based on where these cables are used for installation. I have developed and improved insulations that are used for domestic and commercial wiring application.

Optical fibre coatings

Similar to electrical wires, fibre optics drives our internet and telecommunication network around the globe. These fibres are glass strands which are fragile and need external polymer coatings for commercial applications. These special coatings are sometimes custom made to suit specific fibres and applications. I have worked with commercial coatings manufacturers to meet specific needs of these fibres.
